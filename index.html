About Kazakhstan

Kazakhstan is the ninth-largest country in the world, famous for its vast steppes, nomadic culture, and unique blend of traditions and modernity. From the ancient Silk Road to the Baikonur Cosmodrome, Kazakhstan connects past and future, East and West.
